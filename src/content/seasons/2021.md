2021 marked the inaugural season of Sixth City Dynasty, with 14 franchises convening for a 32-round startup draft in July and kicking off their first slate of games that September. Vancouver and Gold Coast pulled the trigger on the first post-draft trade in Sixth City history on August 11th, setting the tone for a trade block that would become one of the busiest and most exciting places in the league.

Montreal and Bikini Bottom separated themselves from the rest of their conferences, claiming the first ever SCC and HCC titles, respectively. The postseason was a different story, though, as Choccolocco and Vancouver navigated their conference brackets to meet in the very first championship game. The Railfans ultimately outlasted the Mounties and achieved victory on MNF to become Sixth City's first-ever league champion.
