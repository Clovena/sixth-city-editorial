The Lasercats' chance to prove they're more than just Bikini Bottom's farm system. Since entering the league in 2024, Lower City has garnered a reputation of being the minor league Bikini Bottom Stars. This reputation isn't all accurate, as the Lasercats have seen plenty of success themselves, but they've yet to make a true mark in the postseason. Lower City is looking to finish the job they started in earnest in 2024; Bikini Bottom has nothing more to prove after becoming two-time champions.
