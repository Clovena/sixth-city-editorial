 A marquee matchup inspired by the Atomic Age. The Bikini Bottom Stars are based in the Bikini Atoll, the canonical home of SpongeBob SquarePants and the site of a score of nuclear tests between 1946 and 1958. The Winnipeg Wranglers share a home with one of the largest nuclear test sites in Western Canada. The Atom Bowl acknowledges the nuclear legacy of both regions and derives its name from the real-life Atom Bowl played in 1946 in the aftermath of Nagasaki.
