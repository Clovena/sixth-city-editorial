 Two well-acquainted HCC rivals in a clash of overlooked locales. Mississauga lives in the shadow of a larger Canadian metro, and Lower City (Connecticut) remains permanently overlooked in its area. Both have channeled that chip-on-the-shoulder energy year after year. The Cup itself takes its name from the waterfalls common to each team's region, as well as the cascading ambitions of two franchises still hunting their first taste of real success.
