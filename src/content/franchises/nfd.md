The Newfoundland Blowers have been a member of Sixth City since the inaugural season in 2021. The franchise draws its identity from the province's notoriously harsh Atlantic winds that batter the eastern coast, while the caribou logo honors Newfoundland and Labrador's iconic woodland caribou. 

The Blowers operate with a frenetic energy on the trade block, constantly wheeling and dealing in pursuit of the next big thing. While the front office demonstrates a keen eye for talent, their approach lacks long-term vision, occasionally resulting in moves that hinder rather than help. Newfoundland embraces an all-action philosophy, injecting themselves into every opportunity regardless of competitive timeline. This spirited, somewhat chaotic identity has made the Blowers one of the most entertaining franchises to follow in the SCC.
